Welcome to the Inter-hemispheric Brain Proteome Map (IBPM)

Brain Lateralization is an important branch of neuroscience and essential to understand the function of brain, which is the most complex organ of the human body. This portal is a part of a proteomics study involving multiple regions of the brain and their Inter-hemispheric comparisons. This portal allows you to search for proteins found in our study and get an overview of its expression, peptide information, and get information about inter-hemispheric comparison analysis, found through our study.

The IBPM Portal is dynamic and scalable. This portal is based on Django Framework and front-end is developed using Bootstrap V4
